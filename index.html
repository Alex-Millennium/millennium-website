Our Story

25 Years of Quality Homes

Founded in 2000, Millennium Investments 2000 Ltd began with a simple mission: to provide quality, affordable accommodation to students in Colchester. Over 25 years, we've grown into one of the region's most trusted property specialists — managing a diverse portfolio of student HMOs and private residences across Colchester and Ipswich.

We believe every tenant deserves a home they're proud of — with transparent pricing, genuine support, and a management team that truly cares. That ethos has never changed since day one.
